Kygo and Manhle's Starry Night

One clear night, Kygo and Mahle decided to explore the magical forest right outside their home. They put on their cozy pajamas and followed the twinkling lights that floated through the trees.

In the forest, they met a friendly owl named Olly. Olly gave them each a glowing lantern that shone brightly and helped them see the path. Together, they walked to a hidden glade where the stars seemed to dance just above their heads.

Kygo and Mahle lay on the soft grass, watching the stars twirl and sparkle. They heard stories from the stars about brave explorers and fantastic adventures. They made wishes and dreamed of flying on the back of a gentle dragon.

As the night went on, Olly the owl guided them back home. They thanked him for the magical night and promised to visit again. Snuggled up in their beds, Kygo and Manhle drifted off to sleep, dreaming of their starry adventure and the new friends they made.
